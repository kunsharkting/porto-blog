Keramique Script friendly cursive font, designed with a smooth feel and playful look. Its flowing lines make a wonderful lettering, perfect for titling and logotypes. 

Keramique Script has a solid baseline with a comfortably high x-height, providing for a bold text that's easily read. It is perfect for logo branding, or perhaps on a menu for a diner.

Use underscore _ to make a swash under any word.
Example: Hea_rt

Use multiple underscores to make longer underlines. 
Example: Hand___made

The font is built with advanced OpenType functionality and guaranteed top-notch quality, containing stylistic and contextual alternates, ligatures and more automatic and manual features; all to give you full control and customizability. 
It has extensive lingual support, covering all Latin-based languages, from North Europa to South Africa, from America to South-East Asia. It contains all characters and symbols you'll ever need, including all punctuation and numbers. 

This font was made by Mans Greback with a smooth and bold expression, with the objective to craft a playful script that still maintains legibility.
