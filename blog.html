Welcome to our Porto travel blog

As part of our language study trip to Porto, we created this blog to share our daily experiences. From classes and cultural discoveries to city walks and fun moments with friends, you’ll find the highlights of our adventure right here.

Each post includes a summary of the day, a few photos, and maybe even some stories, not all of them entirely serious.

Enjoy reading — and feel free to check back daily to follow our journey.

Day 1 – Exploring the City

We explored the historic center and enjoyed our first Portuguese meal together.

Day 2 – Cultural discovery and Agile methodology

The day started with an engaging class on the Portuguese language and local customs. It was a great opportunity to learn more about the local culture and better understand our surroundings. In the afternoon, we focused on a more technical topic with an introduction to the Agile methodology, which is very useful in the field of IT. After class, we took advantage of the nice weather to walk around the streets of Porto, explore its charming alleys, and enjoy the city’s warm atmosphere.

Day 3 – From HTML to holograms

This morning, we had an introductory course on web development with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. It was a great opportunity to review the essential basics for building a website. In the afternoon, we visited the Néonia museum in Porto—an immersive and enriching experience that blends technology and contemporary art. A great way to end the day.

In today’s photo, you can see our awesome squad: Maxence, Romain, Alexandre and me (from right to left). Future web developers… or maybe fashion models—we’re still deciding !

Day 4 – Douro River Tour

We took a boat tour on the Douro River and learned about Porto’s famous wine.

Day 5 – Visit to Livraria Lello

We visited the famous Livraria Lello bookstore, a must-see for Harry Potter fans!

Day 6 – Group Activities

We participated in group activities and games to practice our English skills.

Day 7 – Free Time in Porto

We had free time to explore Porto’s shops, cafes, and beautiful streets.

Day 8 – Visit to Serralves Museum

We visited the Serralves Museum of Contemporary Art and its stunning gardens.

Day 9 – Cooking Workshop

We learned how to cook traditional Portuguese dishes in a fun workshop.

Day 10 – Farewell Party

We celebrated the end of our trip with a farewell party and shared our favorite memories.

Day 11 – Departure

We packed our bags and said goodbye to Porto, taking home unforgettable memories.
